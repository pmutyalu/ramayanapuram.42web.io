What is Poly Culture ?

Polyculture can be defined as the simultaneous cultivation of more than one crop. In the context of aquaculture, polyculture is the process of producing two or more species in the same pond or physical space at the same time. The main objective of polyculture is to increase the economic value of a crop. Polyculture could mean a combination of animals, plants, or a combination of plants and animals. Polyculture originated in China and spread to all South Asian countries over the years.
